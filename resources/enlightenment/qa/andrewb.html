Enlightenment Questions - Question From Andrew B

Dear Tathagata,

I am in conflict with myself.

Part of me is the ever present watcher, calm, understanding and wise.

The other part of me is the ever present pain, the suffering, the need, the ego.

How can I break this conflict and be my true self ?

I feel this true self state is near, and that I have reached a point where I need to make a transition.

Andrew B

Answer from Tathagata

Dear Andrew,

The answer of your question is simple. If yourself opens eyes to reality, all of what you've had are going to solve. However, I would like to point out that so far you don't know how to open eyes to reality and you didn't deliberate about that for a long time and your effort is not enough. First, you start to endeavor, then you ask me again, I can answer your question better than now.

Tathagata
