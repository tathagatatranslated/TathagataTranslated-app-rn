Enlightenment Questions - Question From SWP

Master Tathagata,

Experiencing great knowledge by Lucid dream, would you say that you had
some experiences of it too time to time?

Sincerly,

S.W.P.

Answer from Tathagata

Dear SWP,

I seldom dream unlike ordinary people, so I don't have any experience that you have got. You dream less when you are more healthy.

Tathagata
