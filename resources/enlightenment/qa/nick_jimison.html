Enlightenment Questions - Question From Nick Jimison

Dear Tathagata,

What are the powers of an enlightened being?

Do you as an enlightend being have the ability to materialize objects or transport yourself?

sincerly,

Nick Jimison

Answer from Tathagata

Dear Nick,

It's truth.

I don't have a help of power from others. I only see what is and distinguish truth and false, and then let people know about that. I always try to inform that people is deceived by what kind of things. In the reality, only one who knows oneself can be helpful to save one's life.

Tathagata
