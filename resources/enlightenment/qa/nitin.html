Enlightenment Questions - Question From Nitin

Dear Tathagata,

I trust you because I have seen the same things which you mention in your prophecy about the end of the

world... Really wish to meet you!! Can we meet ? My name is Nitin and wish to meet you..

How can I reach you ?

Regards

Nitin

Answer from Tathagata

Dear Nitin,

Our meeting depends on your decision. Your will and effort are the bridge between us.

Tathagata
