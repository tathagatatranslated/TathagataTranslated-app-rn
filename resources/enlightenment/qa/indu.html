Enlightenment Questions - Question From Indu

Respected Tathagata

What is the relation between karma and love ? Does getting hurt by a loved one indicate bad karma in the past or does it mean a something better awaiting one in the future ?

Indu

Answer from Tathagata

Dear Indu,

Karma is what is in your motae (origin) which becomes to exist by what you've done in your past. Love is that you accept something to bless the world and tell for others and practice it. Therefore love is the only key to remove karma and the karma never been vanished without love. This is the relationship of them.

Tathagata
