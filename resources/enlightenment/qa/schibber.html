Enlightenment Questions - Question From Schibber

Dear Tathagata,

What is the cause of disease and how can it be overcome ?

Schibber

Answer from Tathagata

Dear Schibber,

A cause of disease can be occured by many things. So, no one can avoid disease. But if you try to live healthy then it can be helpful to protect yourself from disease.

Tathatgata
