Tathagatha: I am very glad to meet you today. May I ask you something?

Seongbon: Sure.

Tathagatha: Then, can you tell me what you are teaching to people around you.

Seongbon: I teach on the Scripture, commandments and meditation in Buddhism.

Tathagatha: Are the contents of the Scripture what Buddha tried to teach, or what religions invented?

Seongbon: I think they are from Buddha.

Tathagatha: Can you distinguish between what someone with eyes open said and what the blind said?

Seongbon: Only Buddha can do that.

Tathagatha: Then, do you know that it can be dangerous to yourself or other people in some aspects for you to teach things in the Scripture?

Seongbon: What do you teach?

Tathagatha: I teach conscience, justice and love.

Seongbon: So do I.
