Wanzala: I am a Christian.

Tathagatha: In Christianity, what do they teach?

Wanzala: They teach the words of God.

Tathagatha: What do you mean by the words of God?

Wanzala: The words of God are in the Bible.

Tathagatha: If you are a Christian you must have read all the contents of the Bible, mustn’t you? What is the main content in the Bible?

Wanzala: It is love.

Tathagatha: What do you mean by love?

Wanzala: Love cannot be explained.

Tathagatha: Why?

Wanzala: It is because only God is aware of love.

Tathagatha: If so, hasn’t God ever taught the very love to you yet?
