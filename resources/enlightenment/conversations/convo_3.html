Vaswani: There are lots of people in the world who insist that they are enlightened.

Tathagatha: Then, have you ever met anyone that says they are enlightened?

Vaswani: No, I have not.

Tathagatha: Even though you haven’t met directly anyone that they say so how can you say so?

Vaswani: Have you ever met an enlightened being?

Tathagatha: No, I have not.

Vaswani: If you meet the enlightened being how can you recognize whether he is enlightened or not?

Tathagatha: Firstly I ask some questions and through the answers I can notice if he is enlightened.

Vaswani: Then, could you ask me the questions you have asked people?

Tathagatha: I have been waiting for you saying so. By the way, I’m wondering one thing before I ask you questions?

Vaswani: What are you wondering?

Tathagatha: At this moment, I wonder what questions can be answered by you when I ask them. So, if you know anything, tell me just one thing among them. Then I will ask about it.

Vaswani: I don’t know anything. You are the true enlightened being.
