Tathagatha: Are you Buddhadhasa?

Buddhadhasa: Yes, I am.

Tathagatha: What does the name, Buddhadhasaa mean?

Buddhadhasa: It means following Buddha.

Tathagatha: If so, what kind of person do you think Buddha is and if a Buddha appears in front of you how can you recognize whether or not he is Buddha?

Buddhadhasa: … ….

Tathagatha: I say I am a Tathagatha and have you ever heard the word, Tathagatha?

Buddhadhasa: You are not a Tathagatha.

Tathagatha: Then if I show you the evidences I have, can you recognize them?

Buddhadhasa: You are not a Tathagatha.

Tathagatha: If you ever meet Buddha you will not be able to follow him.
