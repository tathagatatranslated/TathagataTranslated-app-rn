New world appeared in my calm mind.

I saw myself

Who did not have anguish nor fantasy.

in my lonely heart.


    Blessing 1

All things in the world are teachers.

When I try facing each one,

There is teaching also in the meeting and separating.

All things have favor

And pleasure is in me ,

So blessing is

What keeps and behaves like farming.


    Meaning of heaven and earth

Brought me up humbly

Was for me using for the world,

Made me live with lonely mind

Was letting me know the great meaning of heaven.

When I was seeing hundreds million of aeons time,

It lets me forget the favor and suffering.


    Cause and effect

Mind makes world;

World makes mind.

I am the master of the world

The world is my teacher.

When I and the world meet each other,

It lets me know the past and the future.


    Good deeds 1 (The essence of Buddha)

Conscience must be like a high mountain

And courage must reach heaven.

Accordingly as I go over and over a mind of suffering,

A single beautiful mind blooms in the world.
