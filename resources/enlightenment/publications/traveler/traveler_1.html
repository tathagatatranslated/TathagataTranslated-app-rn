    The Poem dedicated to Two Statues of Goddesses

The whispering is more beautiful

Than your appearance.

You are the soul

Dedicated to justice for freedom indeed.

Your breath is in the brilliant culture

And attract our attention passing by.

Because you have the dream

That you always want to live peacefully,

The appearance is more beautiful and brilliant.


    Wish

Dream.

Let me dream

Peace and love tonight.

Angels sang a song for us,

Human beings built the dream of heaven,

All of us became the savior of the world,

Let me dream such dream.


    Favor

There is a favor in the world

And the meaning is profound.

Burning my heart for others,

My mind is shining.

Harmony of heaven and earth being as much as strict,

Cause and occasion of everybody is different.

When I see a heavy burden by myself,

I feel responsible for all the sins of the world.
