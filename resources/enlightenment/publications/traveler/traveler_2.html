Where should I meet the true world.

I did not have the place to ask it.

I would rather have wished

The dark time was dream.

House of illusion

There is no lover

But the name is .

There is no truth

But the sound is.

Having built the house of illusion

A man who invited the blind.

There is hidden disaster

In the stagnant water.


The word that told me to abandon justice

My feelings are very bad now.

It happened this morning.

My wife told me to forget everything.

But I could not forget a single word.


Evil destiny

Human beings!

Do not make me speak of

Courage and truth now.

Too exhausted to wait,

I became another person.

Getting away from a god of destiny

That is building an evil destiny,

I am hiding myself in the crowd of open-eyed blinds,

Even though you can see me now,

Do not say that you know me.


Mirage

Bigger than the shout of the Jews

And stronger than the rage of the German.

Having built an evil destiny and dancing!

Do you know the secret of mirage?


Sound

Having heard the sound of time

That drove away darkness at the pitch-black night,

Somebody dreams of a poet.

Oh,

Silence!

As if the fire of the heart went out,

The song of destiny is heard in the dark world.

Night!

Night!

Deep night!

Now I close my eyes

And try to sleep,

Do not wake up

Despair in my mind.
