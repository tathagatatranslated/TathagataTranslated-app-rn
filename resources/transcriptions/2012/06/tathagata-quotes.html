Tathagata Quotes

June 11 2012, 3:24 PM  by The Teaching of Tathagata

Will use this space as a collection of quotes I wrote down while listening to Tathagata -Editor

 

If we don't have a good society, nobody can be guaranteed to be happy.

The most important thing in (study) learning is to find what is truth.

When the age changes, then the learning also changes.

During a talk at the hotel while preparing to go out for the day

Tathagata: Satto, if Soyun shows a little bit strange behavior, then stop working
Satto: Stop working – Okay
T: In this world whenever we do something good the evils always make problems. It is difficult for us to overcome their obstacles with our power. Only I can resist (prevent) them from giving damage. This work seems very easy but not any one can do this mission. Yesterday the monk told us that it was a fact – he told
Soyun: him [Tathagata]
T: that, "You are Buddha in the future (you are messiah)".* – That's true!
* [Note: Tathagata is referring to a monk who, in passing, ridiculed the master with this sentence.]
*Satto laughs*
T: So they will never come near a living Buddha. In spite of their neglect, we try to meet them and we visit them. This is mercy and love.
